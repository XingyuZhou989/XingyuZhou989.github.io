About

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering of The Ohio State University, advised by Prof. Ness Shroff. I am broadly interested in cloud computing, networking, optimization and energy efficient communications.

I graduated from BUPT with a B.S. and Tsinghua Univeristy with a M.S., both in Electrical Engineering with honor.
